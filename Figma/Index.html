The Collection Of Figma

What is a Figma

The figma (フィグマ) series is a Japanese action figure line produced by Max Factory and distributed by Good Smile Company. Each figma is sold with various accessories such as exchangeable faces, hands and other optional parts.

The Differents between Figma and Common Figure

The Differents is that The main difference is that Figma figures come in a package with their production number, several interchangeable faces, and also have more joints than other figures on the market.
